Lyric Ninja (Coming Soon)

Create your game under src/lyric-ninja/, mirroring the structure of src/game/:

src/
  lyric-ninja/
    game.html    (entry HTML)
    game.js      (Phaser config)
    scenes/      (your scenes)
    assets/      (sprites/audio/etc)

Then update this file to point to your entry page (for example, replace its contents with a redirect to ./lyric-ninja/game.html or load your bundler output).
